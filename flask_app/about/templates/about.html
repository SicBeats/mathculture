    ABOUT US:

    Who is mathculture?

    We are a group of computer science students at the University of Oregon, attempting to make learning math more fun and interactive.
    Prioritizing learning over cheating, our product is targeted at schools wanting to make learning math more interactive for their students,
    by providing step-by-step solutions for designated math equations.
